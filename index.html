Piano Cubes

Stimulate Your Hears

Piano Cubes is an educational music game. After clicking on the play button, the user has to listen the musical note randomly played by the computer.
Three levels of difficulty are available. If the user guesses correctly, the computer will play another extra note. Otherwise, the user will loose a life.
Technology Used: HTML, 3D CSS, Javascript, Sound Manager, Heroku

Uply

Invite Your Friends

Uply is an online social network dedicated to make in real life interractions with your friends easier.
Instead of texting your friends in group messages or creating Facebook events to gather your mates, ask them simply if they want to join you for any occasion such as a drink, sushi or a football game.
Technology Used: Rails, Ruby, PostgreSQL, Foundation, Javascript, Heroku
